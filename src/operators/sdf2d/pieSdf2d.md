SDF for a 2D pie-slice shape.

## Parameters

* `Radius`: The distance from the center to the outer edge.
* `Angle`: The width of the slice in degrees.
* `Rotate`: Rotation for the slice in degrees.
* `Shape`
  * `slice`
  * `ring`
  * `infinite`
* `Innerradius`
