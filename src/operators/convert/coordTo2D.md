Converts a 3D (or 1D) operator to work in a 2D plane on the chosen axes.

## Parameters

* `Enable`
* `Mode`
  * `custom`
  * `xy`
  * `yx`
  * `yz`
  * `zy`
  * `xz`
  * `zx`
* `Planeoffset`
* `Partx`
  * `x`
  * `y`
  * `zero`
* `Party`
  * `x`
  * `y`
  * `zero`
* `Partz`
  * `x`
  * `y`
  * `zero`
