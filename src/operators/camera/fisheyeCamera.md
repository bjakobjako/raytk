A 360 fisheye camera, that shows all directions from a specific point in space.

## Parameters

* `Fisheyemode`
  * `pinhole`
  * `stereographic`
  * `equiangular`
  * `equisolidangle`
  * `orthographicfisheye`
* `Aperture`
* `Campos`: Position of the camera.
* `Camrot`: Rotates the camera in XYZ.
* `Lookatpos`: Coordinates that the camera should face.
* `Camup`
* `Inspect`
